Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Grocery on Wheels and Inspired Grow.

Grocery on Wheels brings mobile grocery vans to your neighborhood on scheduled routes. You can shop directly from the van or pre-order via our upcoming app for pickup at a designated stop.

We accept cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, and mobile payment apps like Google Pay and PhonePe. Online payments will be available through our app soon.

Local kirana stores and suppliers can partner with us by contacting our team. Email support@inspiredgrow.in or fill out the form below to start the onboarding process.

If you face any issues with your order, please contact our support team via the form below or email support@inspiredgrow.in. We’ll resolve it promptly.

GOW is currently available in Bengaluru, with plans to expand to other cities soon. Check our app or website for the latest updates on locations.

Van schedules are posted on our website and will be available on our app. You can also contact support for real-time updates on van locations.
