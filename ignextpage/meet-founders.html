Founder Profiles

Keshav Singla, Founder

Keshav Singla brings a sharp entrepreneurial mindset shaped by his background in commerce and over 8 years of hands-on experience in retail marketing and vendor onboarding for major e-commerce platforms. Growing up amidst the struggles of small retailers, Keshav developed a strong vision—to bridge the opportunity gap between local vendors and organized commerce.

At Inspired Grow Pvt. Ltd., he leads strategic innovation, drives technology-enabled ventures like Grocery on Wheels, and focuses on building scalable, sustainable platforms that uplift local economies. His mission is rooted in empowering grassroots entrepreneurs with digital tools and market access.

Message from Keshav

"We’re here to bridge the gap between small vendors and large opportunities. Inspired Grow is not just a company—it's a catalyst for inclusive growth and sustainable commerce across India. My dream is to become the second late Shri Ratan Tata—building businesses that empower millions and leave a legacy of compassion and innovation."

Ashish Singla, Founder

Ashish Singla brings over 25 years of expertise in sales, marketing, and business development, with a strong foundation built through years of experience in the printing and manufacturing industries. Known for his strategic foresight and executional discipline, Ashish plays a vital role in operations and expansion planning at Inspired Grow.

With a deep understanding of on-ground logistics and market behavior, Ashish oversees vendor partnerships, supply chain integration, and retail optimization. His leadership ensures that every initiative—from Grocery on Wheels to future ventures—is grounded in operational excellence and community relevance.

Message from Ashish

"At Inspired Grow, we don’t just build ventures—we solve real problems. My focus has always been on execution, sustainability, and impact. Together, we’re laying the foundation for a smarter, more inclusive future for Indian commerce."

Our Path

Their Journey

2017: Seeds of Hustle

Before founding Inspired Grow, Keshav and Ashish immersed themselves in grassroots business challenges. Working independently across retail and supply chain roles, they gained real-world experience and built deep market insights. These early hustles shaped their understanding of the challenges faced by local vendors and consumers alike.

2018: Learning Through Setbacks

Their first entrepreneurial venture, though ambitious, fell short due to funding gaps and infrastructure constraints. Rather than deter them, this setback instilled vital lessons in resilience, strategic execution, and customer-centric design—paving the way for something greater.

2021: Birth Of Inspired Grow Pvt. Ltd.

Armed with hard-earned insights, the duo launched Inspired Grow Pvt. Ltd. with a mission to create scalable, tech-enabled ventures that empower local communities and simplify daily living. The company was born out of purpose, not just profit.

2021: Launch Of Grocery on Wheels-- First Van

Inspired Grow rolled out its flagship initiative—Grocery on Wheels, a mobile retail solution that brought daily essentials directly to customers’ doorsteps. This innovative van-based model disrupted traditional grocery delivery by merging convenience, accessibility, and community reach.

2023: Second Retail Van Deployed

With increasing traction and strong word-of-mouth, operations scaled up with the launch of a second delivery van. This expansion significantly increased service coverage, enabling the team to serve a broader base of residential communities efficiently.

2025: Funding Drive Intiated

To capitalize on growing market potential, Inspired Grow launched its first formal funding campaign. The goal: to enhance logistics, scale technology infrastructure, and accelerate multi-city growth of Grocery on Wheels and future ventures.

2025: Grocery On Wheel App(Comming Soon)

In a move to boost digital convenience, the Grocery on Wheels mobile app is set to launch—equipped with features like live tracking, seamless ordering, personalized offers, and instant support. This will enhance the customer experience while streamlining operations.
