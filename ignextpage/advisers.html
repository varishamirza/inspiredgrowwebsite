Our Mentors

Guiding Our Growth With Experience and Expertise

Aditya Bajaj

Startup & Strategy Mentor

Aditya Bajaj is the founder of Acharya Ventures and a seasoned startup coach with a proven track record of nurturing high-growth businesses. As a serial entrepreneur, Aditya brings invaluable insights to Grocery on Wheels in areas like business strategy, market expansion, and scaling growth operations. His mentoring focuses on refining our strategic vision, optimizing business models, and fostering sustainable growth as we expand into new regions.
Aditya’s mentorship enables Inspired Grow to navigate competitive markets and capitalize on emerging opportunities with precision.

Dr. Gaurav Kumar

Ethics & Customer Relations Mentor

Dr. Gaurav Kumar is a General Physician and former Chief Medical Officer with deep expertise in ethical business practices and customer relations. His mentorship ensures that Grocery on Wheels and other ventures operate with integrity, prioritize customer experience, and build sustainable business models rooted in trust.
Through his guidance, we emphasize ethical marketing, responsible service delivery, and nurturing long-term relationships with our customers, laying the foundation for brand loyalty and a reputation for excellence.

Vikas

Technology Mentor

Vikas is a technology veteran with over 12 years of experience in software development, specializing in creating scalable, reliable, and efficient tech infrastructures. As the Technology Mentor at Inspired Grow, Vikas provides critical guidance to our tech teams, helping them build systems that ensure seamless operations and future-proof solutions for Grocery on Wheels and other digital ventures.
His expertise in cloud computing, data management, and system integration ensures that our platforms remain robust, agile, and scalable as we grow.

Amit

FMCG & Quality Control Mentor

Amit has over 12 years of experience in Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) and quality control. He mentors Inspired Grow on ensuring product quality, efficiency in supply chains, and maintaining high standards across all operations.
His guidance in quality assurance and process optimization ensures that Grocery on Wheels offers the best possible products and services to its customers, creating a reliable and trusted brand reputation in the market.

Our mentors have played a pivotal role in helping Inspired Grow scale key initiatives like Grocery on Wheels, turning local markets into a nationwide network. Empowering Teams

Through their deep expertise in technology, startup strategy, quality control, and customer relations, our mentors empower Inspired Grow’s teams to innovate and perform at their best. By fostering a culture of resilience, creativity, and operational excellence, they ensure that our team is equipped to tackle challenges and drive the success of every venture.
