Grocery on Wheels

Our flagship venture, Grocery on Wheels, transforms the way people shop for daily essentials by bringing mobile grocery vans directly to residential neighborhoods. Designed for convenience, affordability, and accessibility, this service is a lifeline for busy families, elderly citizens, and underserved communities. With real-time inventory management, planned city routes, and vendor integration, we’ve built a scalable model that bridges the gap between kirana stores and modern consumer demand.

Smart Retail Enablement

We empower local retailers with tools to grow digitally. From POS integrations and smart inventory systems to digital training and onboarding for kirana partners, our retail solutions enhance efficiency, profitability, and customer reach. Our goal is to make every small store smart, competitive, and future-ready by blending technology with traditional retail values.
