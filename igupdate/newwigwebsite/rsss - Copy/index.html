Grocery on Wheels

India’s Smartest Mobile Grocery Store

Grocery on Wheels is the flagship venture of Inspired Grow Pvt. Ltd., designed to revolutionize daily shopping in urban and semi-urban neighborhoods. It brings the entire kirana store experience directly to the customer’s doorstep through a tech-enabled mobile retail van, offering convenience, speed, and trust.

Why It Stands Out

Mobile Convenience

Fully-equipped grocery vans that serve gated societies and busy localities with daily essentials.

Real-Time Inventory

Customers can shop in real-time from our live-stocked vans without needing an app.

Women-Friendly Jobs

Focused on empowering local women through onboard store management opportunities.

Tech-Driven

GPS tracking, digital billing, route planning, and soon—a fully integrated customer app.

Local Impact

Supports local FMCG brands, suppliers, and nearby consumers—fueling neighborhood economies.

Built for Scale

From pilot launches to rapid city-level expansion, designed to serve thousands of households across India by 2025.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

2021
Company Founded

Inspired Grow Pvt. Ltd. was established with the vision to create scalable, impactful ventures that simplify everyday living and empower local businesses.

2021
Grocery On Wheel Launched

We launched our flagship project, Grocery on Wheels, bringing daily essentials directly to residential communities through a mobile retail van model.

2023
Second Retail Van Deployed

With growing demand and success, we expanded operations by introducing our second delivery van, doubling our reach and customer base.

2025
Funding Drive Intiated

We began our official funding journey to scale operations, upgrade technology, and grow our venture across multiple cities.

2025
Grocery On Wheel App(Coming Soon)

To enhance customer convenience, our dedicated mobile app is set to launch—bringing order tracking, offers, and seamless shopping right to your fingertips.
